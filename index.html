My name is Jonah Goldfine and I am a post-master's astronomy researcher. I am both on the astronomy and data science job market as well as Domestic and International PhD market. Broadly my interests lie in Stars, Planets, and their systems.

Background

I am originally from Scottsdale, Arizona where I lived until moving for college at New York University. I began at NYU as a Chemistry Major, but not far into my sophomore year I switched to Physics while also choosing to minor in Astronomy. After completing undegrad at NYU and deciding that I was not quite ready to apply to PhD Programs, I started a remote Master's in Data Science at the University of Denver. During this continued my research and worked as an Adjunct Instructor at NYU teaching both major and non-major Physics Recitations and Labs.
